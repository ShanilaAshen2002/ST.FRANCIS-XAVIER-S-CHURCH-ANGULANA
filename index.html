Our Patron Saint

St. Francis Xavier

St. Francis Xavier was one of the greatest Catholic missionaries in history. Known for his tireless zeal and profound love for the Gospel, he traveled extensively across Asia to spread the message of Christ.

As the patron saint of our church, his legacy of faith, courage, and dedication continues to inspire our community in Angulana. We strive to follow his example in living our faith boldly and sharing God's unconditional love with everyone.

About Our Parish

Located in the heart of Moratuwa, St. Francis Xavier's Church has been a beacon of faith and community for generations. Our heritage is deeply rooted in the coastal traditions of Angulana, fostering a spirit of unity and devotion.

We welcome everyone to be part of our vibrant family, celebrating our patron saint's legacy of missionary zeal and love for God. Whether you are a long-time parishioner or a visitor, you will find a home here.
